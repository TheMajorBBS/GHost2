Upgrading from an older version of DoorMUD to the current version
=================================================================

If you already have a previous version of DoorMUD installed on your BBS,
upgrading to the newest release is easy.  Simply unzip the new archive to
your old DoorMUD directory, overwriting the files from the older version.  


To see what has changed in this version of DoorMUD, hit R at the DoorMUD
entrance menu to view the full release notes.
